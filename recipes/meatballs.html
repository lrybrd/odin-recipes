Meatball Recipe

Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 1 (2 pound) bag frozen cooked meatballs
  • 1 (14 ounce) can whole berry cranberry sauce
  • 1 (12 ounce) bottle tomato-based chili sauce
  • Â½ lemon, juiced
  • 2 drops hot sauce (such as Tabasco), or more to taste
  1. Step 1: Place meatballs, cranberry sauce, chili sauce, lemon juice, and hot sauce in a slow cooker; stir to evenly distribute sauce.
  2. Step 2: Cook on High until meatballs completely thawed and hot, about 3 hours.
  3. Step 3: Serve.
